Key Services for Battle Ground Rental Properties

Battle Ground rental properties benefit from our full range of services. Drywall and painting work is consistently our top request as units turn over — fresh paint and clean walls are the fastest way to make a property show well for new tenants.

Door and hardware repair is another common need, especially in family rental homes where interior doors and hardware see heavy daily use. We fix sticking doors, replace worn handles and locks, adjust hinges, and install new hardware as needed.

For Battle Ground properties with yards and exterior structures, our exterior repair service addresses fence gates, trim, siding touch-ups, and weatherproofing. Maintaining curb appeal and structural integrity protects both your property value and tenant satisfaction.

We also provide safety compliance services including smoke detector and CO alarm installation, which is required under Washington state law for all rental properties.
