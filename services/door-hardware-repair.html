Keeping Rental Doors and Hardware in Working Order

Doors and hardware take more abuse in rental properties than almost any other component. Between tenant turnovers, daily use, moving furniture through doorways, and the general wear of rental living, doors develop problems — they stick, latches fail, hinges loosen, and locks wear out. For property managers and landlords, these issues affect both security and tenant satisfaction.

Our door and hardware repair service addresses every door-related issue in rental properties throughout Clark County. From simple hinge tightening to complete lock replacements and door frame rebuilds, we restore doors to proper function quickly and affordably. Security hardware like deadbolts and entry locks should be replaced between tenants as a standard practice, and we make that process seamless as part of your turnover make-ready.

For property managers in Vancouver, WA, Camas, and Battle Ground, functional doors and secure locks are non-negotiable for a leasable unit.

Security Updates Between Tenants

Replacing locks between tenants is one of the most important steps in the turnover process. You cannot be certain that outgoing tenants returned all keys, and previous tenants may have shared copies with others. Installing fresh locks protects the new tenant's safety and limits your liability as a property owner.

We install quality deadbolts and entry knob sets that are durable enough for rental use and affordable enough to replace at every turnover. For property managers with multiple units, we can provide consistent hardware across your portfolio for easier key management. This service pairs naturally with our safety and compliance updates for comprehensive tenant-readiness.

Properties in Ridgefield, Hazel Dell, and Salmon Creek all benefit from this straightforward security step.

Repairing Tenant Damage to Doors

Doors and frames are frequent targets of tenant damage. Kicked-in door frames, punched hollow-core doors, pet-scratched surfaces, and broken hinges are all common issues we repair after move-outs. These repairs need to be completed correctly — not just cosmetically patched — because poorly repaired doors compromise security and create ongoing maintenance issues.

We rebuild door frames with longer screws and reinforced strike areas when frames have been damaged by forced entry. For hollow-core doors with holes, we evaluate whether a repair or replacement is more cost-effective and advise you accordingly. Pet damage to door bottoms and surfaces can often be repaired, but severely damaged doors are better replaced.

For properties across Washougal and Clark County, getting door repairs right the first time prevents repeat maintenance calls and keeps your units secure.

Frequently Asked Questions About Door & Hardware Repair

What door repairs do you handle for rental properties in Clark County?

We repair sticking doors, fix misaligned strikes, replace damaged hinges, repair or replace interior doors, fix kicked-in door frames, adjust closet doors, and address any door-related issue that affects function or security in rental units.

Should I replace locks between tenants on my rental property?

Yes. Replacing or rekeying locks between tenants is a recommended security practice and may be required by your lease terms. It protects the new tenant's safety and reduces liability for the property owner.

What types of door hardware do you install?

We install deadbolts, entry knob sets, passage knobs, privacy knobs, door levers, hinges, door stops, kick plates, strike plates, and closet door hardware. We can match existing styles or upgrade to rental-grade hardware.

Can you fix a door that sticks or won't close properly?

Yes. We diagnose whether the issue is caused by hinge problems, frame settling, humidity swelling, or strike plate misalignment, then apply the appropriate fix. Most sticking doors can be corrected in a single visit.

Do you repair sliding closet doors in rental units?

Yes. We repair and adjust sliding closet doors, replace damaged rollers and tracks, and fix bi-fold doors that have come off their tracks. Properly functioning closet doors are important for showings and tenant satisfaction.

How much does it cost to replace locks on a rental property?

The cost depends on the number of locks and the type of hardware you choose. Standard entry lock sets are affordable and we install them quickly. We provide clear pricing before starting.

Can you repair a kicked-in door frame on a rental?

Yes. We rebuild the damaged frame section, install a new strike plate with longer screws for added strength, and refinish the repair to match the surrounding trim.

Do you install peepholes and door viewers?

Yes. Installing peepholes is a quick and affordable security upgrade for rental properties. We drill and install door viewers on entry doors to improve tenant safety.

What areas in Clark County do you serve for door and hardware repair?

We serve all of Clark County including Vancouver, Camas, Washougal, Battle Ground, Ridgefield, Hazel Dell, and Salmon Creek.

Can you fix cabinet hinges and drawer hardware?

Yes. We repair and replace cabinet hinges, drawer slides, pulls, and knobs throughout kitchen and bathroom cabinets. Tightening or replacing loose cabinet hardware improves the feel and function of a rental unit.

Do you handle exterior door repairs for rental properties?

We handle exterior door adjustments, weatherstripping replacement, threshold repairs, and hardware replacement. For structural damage or rot, we assess and recommend the appropriate repair or replacement approach.

Can you install door sweeps and weatherstripping?

Yes. Door sweeps and weatherstripping are important for energy efficiency and keeping out drafts, moisture, and pests. We install these on both interior and exterior doors.

How quickly can you replace locks on a rental after tenant move-out?

Lock replacement is a quick job that can typically be completed within a few hours. We can often include it as part of a turnover make-ready visit.

Do you repair or replace bathroom door locks?

Yes. We install and repair privacy locks on bathroom and bedroom doors. We replace them with reliable hardware that meets the needs of rental living.

Can you fix doors that have been damaged by pets?

Yes. Pet damage to doors is common in rentals. We repair surface damage where possible or replace doors when the damage is too extensive to repair cost-effectively.

What hardware brands do you recommend for rental properties?
