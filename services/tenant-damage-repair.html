Restore Your Rental After Tenant Damage

Tenant damage goes beyond normal wear and tear — it includes holes punched in walls, broken door frames, damaged fixtures, burn marks, pet scratches, unauthorized modifications, and other issues that require repair before the unit can be shown or leased to a new tenant. These repairs need to be completed quickly because every day the unit sits damaged is a day it cannot generate income.

Our tenant damage repair service is built for speed and quality. We assess the damage, confirm scope and pricing with you by email, and complete repairs efficiently so you can move forward with leasing. For property managers in Vancouver, WA and across Clark County, we provide the documented, professional repair service that supports your security deposit process and gets units back on the market.

Common Tenant Damage We Repair

Drywall Damage

Holes, dents, and gouges in walls from impacts, furniture, or unauthorized modifications. We patch, texture-match, and paint. See our drywall and painting service.

Door & Frame Damage

Kicked-in doors, broken frames, damaged hinges, and broken locks. We repair or replace as needed. Learn about our door and hardware repair.

Fixture & Hardware Damage

Broken light fixtures, cracked outlet covers, damaged towel bars, and stripped cabinet hardware replaced and restored.

Pet Damage

Scratched doors, chewed trim and baseboards, damaged blinds, and stained surfaces repaired to pre-pet condition.

Bathroom Damage

Broken towel bars pulling from walls, cracked toilet components, damaged vanity hardware, and mold from ventilation neglect.

Window & Blind Damage

Broken blinds, damaged screens, and window hardware issues repaired or replaced for a clean presentation.

Documentation for Security Deposit Claims

One of the most valuable aspects of our tenant damage repair service is the documentation we provide. We photograph damage before repairs, describe each item clearly, and provide itemized costs for all work performed. This documentation supports your security deposit deduction claims under Washington state landlord-tenant law.

Property managers in Hazel Dell, Salmon Creek, and Ridgefield rely on this documentation for their deposit accounting. Having professional, third-party documentation of damage and repair costs strengthens your position and provides clear evidence of charges beyond normal wear and tear.

Frequently Asked Questions About Tenant Damage Repair

What types of tenant damage do you repair in Clark County?

We repair drywall holes and dents, broken door frames, damaged cabinet hardware, cracked fixtures, stained or damaged surfaces, broken blinds, stripped screws, and other interior damage caused by tenants that goes beyond normal wear and tear.

How quickly can you repair tenant damage in a rental unit?

Most tenant damage repairs can be completed within one to two days depending on scope. We prioritize these repairs because they directly impact your ability to re-lease the unit. Email us photos and a description for a quick timeline estimate.

Can you document tenant damage for security deposit claims?

Yes. We provide detailed documentation including photos and descriptions of damage along with the cost of repairs. This documentation can support security deposit deduction claims and provides clear evidence of damage beyond normal wear and tear.

What is the difference between tenant damage and normal wear and tear?

Normal wear includes minor scuffs, small nail holes, and gradual carpet wear from everyday use. Tenant damage includes holes punched in walls, broken doors or frames, water damage from neglect, burn marks, unauthorized modifications, and other damage caused by misuse or negligence.

Do you repair holes in drywall from tenant damage?

Yes. Drywall repair is one of our most common tenant damage services. We patch holes of all sizes, from small punch holes to large openings, texture-match the repair, and paint to blend with the surrounding wall.

Can you fix doors that tenants have damaged?

We repair and replace damaged interior doors, fix kicked-in door frames, repair broken hinges, and address other door-related damage. For extensive door frame damage, we can rebuild the frame and install a new prehung door as needed.

Do you handle cabinet and countertop damage repairs?

We repair damaged cabinet doors, replace broken hardware, fix drawer slides, and address surface damage where feasible. For extensive countertop damage requiring full replacement, we can assess the scope and provide recommendations.

What areas in Clark County do you serve for tenant damage repair?

We serve all of Clark County including Vancouver, Camas, Washougal, Battle Ground, Ridgefield, Hazel Dell, and Salmon Creek.

How do I get a quote for tenant damage repair?

Email us at service@rental-handyman.com or use the quote form on our homepage. Include the property address, photos of the damage, and a list of items that need repair.

Can tenant damage repair be combined with turnover make-ready?

Absolutely. We frequently combine tenant damage repair with turnover make-ready services. Addressing all items in a single visit is more efficient and gets your unit back on the market faster.

Do you repair bathroom damage caused by tenants?

Yes. Common bathroom damage includes broken towel bars, damaged tile, cracked toilet tank lids, and mold or mildew from poor ventilation habits. We repair and replace these items to restore the bathroom to proper condition.

What about electrical fixture damage from tenants?

We replace broken light fixtures, damaged outlet covers, and switch plates that tenants have cracked or broken. For more complex electrical issues, we will identify the problem and recommend a licensed electrician when the scope requires it.

Can you fix flooring damage caused by tenants?

We handle minor flooring repairs including replacing damaged vinyl planks, fixing transition strips, and addressing surface damage. For extensive flooring replacement needs, we can assess the scope and coordinate with flooring specialists.

How do you handle window and blind damage?

We replace broken blinds, repair window hardware, fix screen damage, and address window-related issues caused by tenants. For broken glass requiring glazing work, we can coordinate with glass specialists.

Do you repair damage to closets and storage areas?

Yes. We repair or replace damaged closet shelving, broken rods, damaged bi-fold doors, and other closet components. These repairs are important for presenting the unit well to prospective tenants during showings.

What if tenant damage is extensive and requires major work?

For extensive damage, we assess the full scope, document everything, and provide a comprehensive repair plan. We handle the handyman-level repairs and coordinate with specialists for any work requiring licensed trades.

Can you match paint colors when repairing tenant damage?

We do our best to match existing paint colors for patch repairs. When the existing paint has faded significantly, we may recommend repainting the entire wall or room for a consistent finish that looks clean and professional.

Do you handle pet damage repairs in rental properties?

Yes. Pet damage is a common issue in rentals and includes scratched doors, chewed trim, stained carpets, and damaged blinds. We repair and replace components damaged by pets to restore the unit to pre-pet condition.

Is tenant damage repair available on short notice?

We do our best to accommodate urgent tenant damage repairs, especially when they affect your ability to re-lease a unit. Contact us as soon as you identify the damage and we will work to fit you into our schedule quickly.

Do you provide itemized invoices for tenant damage repairs?

Yes. We provide clear, itemized documentation of all repairs completed, which is useful for security deposit deductions and owner reporting. Each item is described with the work performed and associated cost.
